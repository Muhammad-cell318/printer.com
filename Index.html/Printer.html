Explanation Of Printers

👉 Laser Printer

Laser Printer is use to toner cartridges to produce high-quality text and images. The speed of printing of laser printer is 100 print in per minute.

👉 Inkjet Printers

Inkjet printers is used for ink cartridges to print text and image. The first ink jet printer was released in 1976 by canon.

👉 3D Printers

3D Printers create three-dimensional object from digital designs. 3D printers has been used to create prosthetif limbs, organs and even house. 3D printers speed vary greatly dependind on the technology, modle, and settings here.FFF(Fused Filament Fabrication), High-end: 150-300 mm/h (5.9-11.8 in/h).

👉 Thermal Printers

Thermal Printer are know for their high-speed printing capabilities. High-speed of Thermal printer is "Up to 20 ips or maore. Some example of thermal printers include. "Zebra zT230:6ips, Epson TM-P20:4ips, Brother PJ-763:4ips, SATO CL4NX:10ips. The thermal printer is used to label printing, barcode printing etc.

👉 Dot Matrix Printers

Dot Matrix printer are oldesr technology, but still used in some niches. The speed of printing is 10-20 ppm. Example of dot matrix is, "Epson LX-300: 160 cps (about 4 ppm), OKI ML3390: 375 cps(about 10 ppm) etc. Dot matrix printer are still used in some applications, such as, "Multi-part forms printing, carbon copy printing, Industrial and commercial, etc.
